Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary


Located 16 km from Kottayam and spread across 14 acres on the banks of the Vembanad Lake is a bird watcher's paradise- the Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary. With a luxuriant avian life that boasts around 180 species of birds, it is the first scientifically formed and preserved bird sanctuary in India.Flocks of birds flitting about and flying across the lake, with the lush mangrove forests and coconut groves forming the backdrop is one breathtaking sight

Illickal Kallu


One of the most sought after destinations in Kottayam is the Illickal Kallu, a majestic hill which has a part of its peak shorn off. Illickal Kallu forms a part of three hills with each rising around 3300 ft. While one hill resembles a mushroom whereby it gets its name as Kuda Kallu (umbrella shaped rock), the second hill carries a small hunch on its sides and is called Koonu Kallu (hunchback rock). 'Neela Koduveli', a medicinal herb which drapes the hillsides in blue is believed to possess supernatural powers grows at Kuda Kallu.

Kattikkayam Waterfalls


This spectacular waterfall straddled by two mountains is located at Mechal near Melukavu in Kottayam. The waters tumbling down the rocky face set amidst the lush green surrounds is an alluring sight. The Kattikkayam Waterfalls is an ideal spot to witness nature in all its splendour and is en-route to Ilaveezhapoonchira from Illickal Kallu.
